# 🧠 Cybersecurity Awareness & Scam Alert Quizzes
This repository provides **scenario-based cybersecurity awareness quizzes**
designed to help **non-technical users** recognise and respond to common cyber
threats.

The format is inspired by **industry-standard security awareness training** and
focuses on practical, real-life decision-making rather than technical skills.
---

## 🎯 Intended Audience
These quizzes are suitable for:
- ✅ Parents and guardians
- ✅ Doctors and healthcare professionals
- ✅ Business and office professionals
- ✅ Students and general internet users
No technical background is required.
---

## 🧩 Quiz Style & Approach
The quizzes follow a **scenario-based learning model**, where users are presented
with realistic situations and asked how they would respond.
Typical scenarios include:
- Suspicious emails or messages
- Online scams and fraud attempts
- Password and account security decisions
- Data privacy and social media risks
Each question includes:
- Multiple-choice answers
- Clear explanation of the correct response
- Simple guidance on safer behaviour
  
---
## 🚨 Scam & Threat Awareness Topics
Planned and ongoing quiz topics include:
- Phishing and email scams
- SMS and WhatsApp fraud
- Phone-based impersonation scams
- Fake delivery and payment requests
- Account compromise warning signs
- Online safety for families and children

---
## ⚠️ Important Notes
- This repository contains **no phishing simulations**
- No impersonation or deceptive activity is performed
- All content is **educational and awareness-focused only**

---
## 🔐 Ethical & Educational Use
The purpose of this material is to:
- Improve cyber risk awareness
- Encourage safer online habits
- Reduce the likelihood of successful scams
- Support informed, responsible technology use
